Bull Spread: An options strategy betting on a rising market. It involves buying a lower-strike call and selling a higher-strike call simultaneously.

Butterfly Spread: Combines bull and bear spreads. It uses three strike prices to create a position with limited risk and profit potential.

Bear Spread: An options strategy for a falling market. It involves buying a higher-strike put and selling a lower-strike put simultaneously.
